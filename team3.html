Bhavna

Bhavna is an enthusiastic recruiter who believes that at the heart of performance and success of any team or organization, lies the quality of its people. In her 17 years of experience, she has worked across India , Singapore and UAE with diverse nationalities and cultures. She has an innate ability to build and nurture relationships which have contributed to her learning & success. Bhavna is an Associate CIPD and a post graduate in Human Resource Management from Xavier’s’ Labour Relations Institute ( XLRI- Jamshedpur) in India. She forayed into executive search while working in Singapore and has, since then, worked with some well-established names in the industry. At Peergrowth, Bhavna is building capability in recruiting for junior to middle management across sectors like Healthcare, Retail, Education, Financial Services, Banking and Hospitality to name a few. Bhavna believes in the power of hard work and perseverance. When not working, she enjoys reading, playing board games, traveling and honing her culinary skills.
