Bhavna Kapoor

Engagement Manager

Bhavna comes from New Delhi, India. She is a recruiter and a networker with a knack for nurturing relationships. While having worked across geographies like India, Singapore and UAE, she has inculcated an exceptional ability to partner closely with people from diverse backgrounds and culture. She takes pride in driving the entire life cycle of recruitment from sourcing, client management till onboarding. She loves connecting with individuals and assisting them in realizing their career aspirations. She believes, “There will be obstacles. There will be doubters. There will be mistakes. But with hard work, there are no limits.”
When not recruiting she is probably building jigsaws, honing her culinary skills, reading or traveling.


Education Qualification:

Associate CIPD, Certificate in HRM from XLRI-Jamshedpur, India


Industry Expertise:

Healthcare, Retail, Education, Financial Services, Banking and Hospitality.
